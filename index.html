SOMEONE

Sometimes I think I am all alone.
Depressed with tonnes of thoughts,
Should I do this or that? confused like a plant supported by a rod ....all the way twined.
I think I am in search of someone who can understand me,
who instead of judging me , support me
who instead of ignoring me, be around me
and just be with me , when I need someone the most
not just vanishing out of blue in the sky like smoke.
And guess what this SOMEONE is still not known
That's why I think I am all alone.


I want to be that butterfly,
Who emerges from the cocoon with beautiful colorful wings
and fly high in the sky.


I want to be that daffodil,
whose beauty is cherished in springs
and whose beautiful yellow color
reflects brightness and zing.


I want to be those giant trees,
who stood like brave soldiers amidst harsh weather and storm
beautiful again in sunshine, purifying the enviroment
doing everything by its norm.


I want to beautifully fly high and be strong.
But my anxiety says my soul...
Hmmm lets just ignore.


Going deep in my thoughts.
in a hope to untangle the mess,
all I see were the junk of unanswered questions
screaming loud for solution.
From those pile of junk a question screamed,
Hey girl!!! are you still in a dream?
"You might be in search of someone but let me tell you we are in search of you"


Little did I know that my problem in already solved.


Looking in the mirror I said to myself,
Hey SOMEONE
Haven't you took so much time.
